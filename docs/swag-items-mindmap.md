## 📊 **Priority Categories for Launch**
1. **T-Shirts** (highest demand, easy fulfillment)
2. **Mugs** (popular, good margins)
3. **Stickers** (low cost, high volume)
4. **Hoodies** (higher value, seasonal)
5. **Tech accessories** (useful, good margins)
